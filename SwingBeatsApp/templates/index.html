Dance studios are losing $71.5 billion yearly from students signing up for classes and dropping within the same month. Learning to dance poses a huge learning curve, and currently, dancers aren’t practicing enough outside of class, to overcome it.

That’s where swingbeats comes in. Created by dancers, for dancers.

We’ve created Haptic Feedback Ankle Bracelets (HFABs) that vibrate on the user's foot to signal dancers when to do a certain step.

These HFABs also provide both dancers and instructors with feedback regarding accuracy and practice time.

In bridging the intersection between technology and dance, we provide our customers with a model that gives them unprecedented insight into their students’ progress.
